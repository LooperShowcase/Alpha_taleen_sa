About BLACKPINK

BLACKPINK is one of the most successful girl groups in the world. Formed by YG Entertainment in 2016, they became global icons representing K-pop on the international stage.

Their name combines “Black” and “Pink” to symbolize both toughness and beauty. BLACKPINK’s influence goes beyond music — they dominate fashion, social media, and brand endorsements.
