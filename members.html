Members

Jisoo (김지수)

Jisoo, born in 1995, is the eldest member and lead vocalist of BLACKPINK. She’s known for her charming visuals, elegant stage presence, and acting roles in dramas like “Snowdrop.” She’s also the global ambassador of Dior.

Jennie (제니)

Jennie, born in 1996, is the main rapper and vocalist. She debuted her solo single “SOLO” in 2018, which became a massive success. Jennie is known for her confidence and fashion sense, representing Chanel worldwide.

Rosé (로제)

Rosé, born in 1997, is the main vocalist and lead dancer. Her voice is unique and emotional, and she released her solo “On The Ground” in 2021. She is also a brand ambassador for YSL and Tiffany & Co.

Lisa (리사)

Lisa, born in 1997 in Thailand, is the main dancer and rapper. She’s known for her sharp dance moves and stage charisma. Her solo “LALISA” broke YouTube records and made her one of the most famous K-pop idols globally.
